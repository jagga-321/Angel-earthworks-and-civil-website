// 02 — Services

What we
actually do.

Three core offerings, all powered by brand new gear. Flexible hire arrangements. 4-hour minimum hire. Local rates on the Fleurieu, regional rates available across SA at cost. No hidden fees.

// 01 — Excavator

Hitachi ZX55U Mini Excavator

Five-tonne mini excavator — small enough to fit through gateways and tight residential blocks, powerful enough for footings, trenching, and serious site prep work.

Hire it three ways: wet hire (machine + me operating), dry hire (you provide your own ticketed operator), or day rate (fixed price for a full shift). 4-hour minimum hire.

Standard Attachments (included)

  • Mud Bucket (large, for clean trimming & bulk)
  • 450mm bucket
  • 300mm bucket

Specialty Attachments (extra cost)

  • Auger Drive (post holes + drilled footings)
  • Rock Breaker (hydraulic hammer)
  • Compaction Wheel (trench compaction)
  • Skeleton Bucket (rock sorting / sifting)
  • Ripper Tooth (rocky ground)
// 02 — Tipper Truck

Hino 700 Tipper

Heavy rigid tipper truck for cartage, spoil removal, aggregate delivery, and bulk material movement. Charged hourly or by day rate.

Local rates apply across the Fleurieu Peninsula. Regional surcharge applies for jobs beyond ~50km from Victor Harbor — fair and transparent.

Typical Uses

  • Soil & spoil removal off site
  • Aggregate, roadbase, sand delivery
  • Rubble & demolition waste cartage
  • Builder's mix & topsoil supply
  • Compatible with our excavator on-site
// 03 — Civil Works

Full Civil & Earthworks

For when you need the job DONE, not just the gear. Quoted projects from site cut through to certified completion.

Project Types

  • House pad preparation & site cut
  • Footing trenches (residential & shed)
  • Driveways & rural access tracks
  • Civil drainage & runoff management
  • Sub-base preparation
  • Backfilling & final landscaping prep

// Residential

Block Prep

Wastewater Digs

Septic tank install, leach drains, transpiration beds, pump wells. Done to plan and to council requirements.

// Outdoors

Pool Digs

In-ground pool excavation. Tight-access capable with our mini excavator. Spoil removal via our tipper.

// Civil

Bulk Earthworks

Larger-scale earth moving, contouring and site shaping. We bring in cartage support as needed for big jobs.

// Footings

Footing Trenches

Strip footings, pad footings, trenching for residential and shed footings. Quick, clean, to-spec.

// Access

Driveways & Tracks

Driveway preparation, rural access tracks, base prep ready for paving or gravel topcoat.

// Stormwater

Drainage

Stormwater runoff, ag drains, subsoil drainage, swales — anything to manage water on your block.

// Walls

Retaining Prep

Excavation and groundwork for retaining walls. Cut the bench, level the base, ready for the wall guys.

// Drilling

Post Hole & Footing Drilling

Auger drive for fence posts, deck footings, pier holes, and drilled structural footings. Clean, accurate, and fast.

// Hard ground

Rock Breaking

Hydraulic rock breaker for hard ground, concrete demolition, rock removal, and stubborn material. Breaks what buckets can't shift.

// Trenches

Trench Compaction

Compaction wheel for backfilled trenches and tight spots — proper compaction without needing a separate plant on site.

// Clearing

Site Clearing

Vegetation clearing, stump removal, ground prep. Ready your block for the next stage.

// Sheds

Shed Pads

Levelled pads for sheds, workshops, carports. Compacted, square and slab-ready.

Spoil & Cartage
