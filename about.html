// 03 — About

Local fella,

Angel Earthworks and Civil is owner-operated by Jamie Angel, based on the Fleurieu Peninsula in South Australia. We're small enough to care about every job — and serious enough to do them right.

// The story

Built on the
Fleurieu.

Angel Earthworks started with one machine, one truck, and one rule: do the work properly, charge what's fair, turn up when you said you would.

We're based out of Victor Harbor and Goolwa, working across the Fleurieu Peninsula and into the wider SA regions. Most of our work is residential and small commercial — home builds, sheds, driveways, drainage, site prep for new constructions.

The earthworks game is full of bigger outfits with bigger overheads. We're not trying to be them. We're trying to be the bloke you call when you want straightforward work by someone who actually gives a stuff about the job.

// How we operate

Four things we
won't compromise on.

Honest Quotes

Every quote is itemised. Hours, rates, attachments, GST — all spelled out. No vague lump sums or surprise extras at invoice time.

Time Respect

On a build, your time is money. If we say 7am Monday, we'll be there 7am Monday. If something changes, you hear it from us first — not when you're waiting on site.

Clean Sites

We leave the site tidier than we found it. Spoil cleared, work area swept, no rubbish left behind. It's our name on the job.

Local Care

We live here. We see our work every day driving past. That keeps us honest in a way an outfit from outside the region never can be.
